How to Play

Friendly guide to this App

Check-in
Just the starting page of the app. Nothing to see here!

Items
Collect them all! Items may be found by "foraging" in the stats page with random chance and loot drops by boss.

Stats
Shows your map. Forage here for items. Forages are renewed to 5 a day. Visit new places for a chance to get more forages!

Character
Shows your character's progress in the game and boost against boss. Boost are increased by number and type of items found!

Boss
Boss will be spawned in a random game area. The 6 game areas are Arts, Business, Computing, SDE, Engineering and Science. You must be in the same area as the boss to fight it! Defeat the boss before the time limit to get items and more forages!

Thank you and enjoy
